Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

This is the personal artist portfolio website of Regina Fröhlich, accessible at www.reginafroehlich.com. The purpose of this website is to showcase original artwork and commissions, and to allow visitors to get in touch.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
Email: info@reginafroehlich.com

2. What Personal Data We Collect and Why

2.1 Server Log Data

When you visit this website, your web hosting provider automatically records technical data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit. This data is collected by the hosting provider for technical maintenance and security purposes. We do not have direct access to or control over this data.

2.2 Direct Contact via Email

If you contact us using the email address listed on this website, any personal information you include in your message (such as your name, email address, or project details) is used solely to respond to your inquiry. This information is not stored in any database beyond our own email inbox and is not shared with any third party.

2.3 Contact via WhatsApp

This website lists a WhatsApp number as an additional contact option. WhatsApp is a messaging service operated by Meta Platforms Ireland Limited, 4 Grand Canal Square, Grand Canal Harbour, Dublin 2, Ireland ("Meta").

If you choose to contact us via WhatsApp:

  • Your messages, including personal information such as your name, phone number, and message content, are transmitted through and stored on WhatsApp’s infrastructure operated by Meta.
  • Meta processes this data in accordance with its own privacy policy: whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • We use WhatsApp solely to communicate with you regarding your inquiry, commission, or artwork interest.
  • We do not share your WhatsApp contact details with any third party.

By initiating contact via WhatsApp, you acknowledge that your data will be processed by Meta as described in their privacy policy.

2.4 Newsletter Subscription via Brevo

This website offers an optional newsletter. If you subscribe, your email address (and any other information you provide during sign-up) will be processed by Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), operated by Brevo SAS, 55 rue d’Amsterdam, 75008 Paris, France.

Brevo acts as a data processor on our behalf. Your data is used exclusively to send you our newsletter and is not used for any other purpose.

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in every newsletter email.

Brevo privacy policy: https://www.brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy/

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Server log data: Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Email contact: Legitimate interest and pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) and (f) GDPR).
  • WhatsApp contact: Voluntary contact, legitimate interest and pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) and (f) GDPR).
  • Newsletter subscription: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data for commercial purposes.

  • Meta (WhatsApp) – processes message data via its own infrastructure.
  • Brevo – processes newsletter data as a data processor on our behalf.

5. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not directly use cookies or tracking technologies. However, the hosting provider may set technically necessary cookies. Brevo may use tracking pixels in newsletters to measure open rates. You can disable image loading in your email client to prevent tracking. WhatsApp may use its own analytics as described in Meta’s privacy policy.

6. Data Retention

  • Server log data is retained according to the hosting provider’s retention policy.
  • Email correspondence is stored only as long as necessary to handle your inquiry.
  • WhatsApp messages are retained as long as reasonably necessary. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Newsletter data is retained by Brevo while you remain subscribed and deleted or anonymized upon unsubscribing.

7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Rectification of incorrect data
  • Deletion (right to erasure)
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, contact: info@reginafroehlich.com

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data we handle directly. Brevo and Meta implement their own industry-standard security measures for data processed on their platforms.

9. Children’s Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has submitted personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We recommend reviewing it occasionally to stay informed about how we protect your data.
